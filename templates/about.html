Our Mission

Helping restore the planet back to its natural beauty.

We aim to do this through raising awareness of deforestation and by planting trees.

The frequent and saddening appearances of the devastating consequences of climate change and deforestation in the media motivated us to create a project that informs as many people as possible about these issues and is accessible to all ages.

We came up with the idea of a game where you plant your own trees to educate our younger audience about how beneficial tree planting is, and how consequences like pollution and global warming can get out of hand if we do nothing about it.

We also created an explore page which is great for raising awareness about deforestation; you can discover new articles and use them to help spread the word of how our way of life needs to change if we want to save our planet.

How can you help us?

Our vision can only be achieved through your support on this website; playing Sonny's Forest, visiting articles in the explore page and directly donating to us in the donate page are all fantastic ways to help our cause. All of your generous donations will go straight to our tree-planting organisation partners such as The Woodland Trust and Tree Aid, so you can be sure that your donation has made an impact.
